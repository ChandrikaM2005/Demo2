Welcome to the World of Doraemon!

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century, sent back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. Created by Japanese manga artists Fujiko F. Fujio, Doraemon is loved by millions around the world.

He has a magical 4-dimensional pocket from which he pulls out various futuristic gadgets to help Nobita overcome everyday problems. Some of his famous gadgets include the "Anywhere Door", "Time Machine", and "Bamboo Copter".

Doraemon's kind heart, humorous personality, and inventive gadgets make him a favorite character for kids and adults alike!
